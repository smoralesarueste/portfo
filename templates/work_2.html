2: University of Chile

June 2019 - June 2021

After gettting my undergrad degree in Industrial Engineering, I started my Master in Operations Research. My thesis (Campañas Electorales Bajos los Ojos de la Teoría de Juegos: Modelos de Comportamiento y Equilibrios, or Electoral Campaigning in the Eyes of Game Theory: Behavior Models and Equilibria) was later published with Professor Charles Thraves.
On finishing my Master, I started working also with Professors José R. Correa and Rafael Epstein and the team of engineers in charge of developing and deploying the software for the School Admission System (Sistema de Admisión Escolar), using matching algorithms to assign half million students from all over the country every year to every public school, according to their declared preferences. I was also in charge of keeping the daily relation with the Ministry of Education (the main stakeholder of the project), tracking the different KPI's and presenting the results to them.
I also kept working with Professor Thraves on Game Theory, Optimization and Statistics as Research Assistant during that time.
